Return on Investment (ROI)

The ROI formula is a key financial metric:

$$ROI = \frac{(Net\ Profit - Total\ Investment)}{Total\ Investment} \times 100$$

Where:

  • $Net\ Profit$ = The gain from an investment
  • $Total\ Investment$ = The cost of the investment
